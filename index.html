General Information

Biography

Victor Junqiu WEI is currently working as a research assistant professor in Department of Computing of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. Prior to this, he was an AI Researcher in Huawei Noah's Ark Lab. He obtained his bachelor degree from Nanjing University and PhD degree from Department of Computer Science and Engineering, the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, under supervision of Prof. Raymond Chi-Wing Wong. From March 2018 to May 2018, he was a visiting student of Prof. Hanan Samet and Prof. David Mount at University of Maryland in US. He has close collaboration with Prof. Raymond Chi-Wing Wong, Prof. Pan Hui, Prof. Cheng Long, Prof. Bo Tang, Prof. Hanan Samet and Prof. David Mount.

Research Statement

His research interests span spatial-temporal data management (Published in SIGMOD 2017, TKDE 2019, SIGMOD 2020, ICDE 2020, SIGMOD 2023, TODS 2022), graph data management (SIGMOD 2020, TKDD 2022), random sampling (ICDM 2019, ICDE 2020), deep neural networks (AAAI 2021, ACL 2022, EMNLP 2022) and natural language processing (AAAI 2021, ACL 2022, EMNLP 2022), esp. pre-trained language modeling. His vision is to optimize the data management and natural language processing with advanced data structures, machine learning and deep learning algorithms. He served as a program committee member of many top conferences such as ICDE, PAKDD, SSTD, COLING, NAACL, EMNLP and ACL, etc.
